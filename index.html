The President leaves the office on Monday, 29 May 2023, 9:00am

President Buhari assumed office on the 29th May 2015 after suceeding Goodluck Jonathan.
He assumed office riding on the wave of fixing insecurity the country was plagued with and still plagued with.
He delivered a presidential speech that had the famous line
"I belong to everybody
And I belong to nobody"

This statement left people in awe of him but the curtain was about to unfold when he made this remark in an interview.
“I hope you have a copy of the election results.
The constituents, for example, gave me 97% [of the vote]
cannot in all honesty be treated on some issues
with constituencies that gave me 5%.”
